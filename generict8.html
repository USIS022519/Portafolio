Physical network installation

Installation of a local area network (LAN) infrastructure in the Computing Lab.

The physical network was installed in the Computer Laboratory at the Gerardo Barrios University, Usuluán campus, from November to December 2022. This project was carried out jointly with the staff of the technology unit of said institution, myself, where I was performing social service, and colleagues, where this type of infrastructure was installed from scratch, which was carried out with the purpose of modernizing the infrastructure of this laboratory.
